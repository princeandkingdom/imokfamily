About

Secret Of I Ching is a modern decision guidance application inspired by the wisdom of the ancient Chinese classic, the I Ching (Book of Changes). Rather than predicting the future, the app encourages thoughtful reflection through structured interpretation, helping users make better personal and professional decisions.

Features

  • Traditional I Ching wisdom
  • Modern decision guidance
  • Private consultation scheduling
  • Secure account management
  • Simple and elegant experience

Privacy

We respect your privacy. User information is collected only when necessary to provide account services, purchases, scheduling and application functionality. We never sell personal information.
