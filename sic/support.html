Frequently asked questions

Is this app a prediction service?

No. The app is designed for reflection and guidance. It does not guarantee outcomes.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the purchase or account area in the app and choose Restore Purchases.

How do I request account deletion?

Email support@imokfamily.com from the address connected to your account.

Does the app provide professional advice?

No. It does not provide medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice.
