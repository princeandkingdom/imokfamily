About

Secret Of I Ching is a modern decision guidance app inspired by the wisdom of the ancient Chinese classic, the I Ching (Book of Changes).

Instead of predicting the future, the app helps users reflect, explore possibilities, and make thoughtful life decisions.

Private consultation scheduling and secure account management are supported within the application.
