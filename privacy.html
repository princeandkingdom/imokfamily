Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how IMOK and IMOK Family collect, use and protect information when you use our mobile applications, website and related services.

1. Information we collect

Depending on the features you use, we may process account identifiers, device information, app activity, approximate or precise location, emergency alert information, motion or fall-related sensor events, connection records between family members, and support communications.

2. How we use information

We use information to provide location sharing, SOS alerts, fall and inactivity notifications, family connections, account support, security, troubleshooting and service improvement.

3. Location and sensor data

Location and motion-related information is used only to provide the safety features you enable. Some features may require background access. Device settings and operating-system permissions control whether this data is available.

4. Sharing

Information may be shared with family members or caregivers you connect to the service, with infrastructure and service providers that help operate IMOK, or when required by law. We do not sell personal information.

5. Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and protect users. Retention periods may vary by data type and account status.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect information. No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices

You may manage app permissions through your device, disconnect family relationships in the app, or contact us to request access, correction or deletion where applicable.

8. Children

IMOK is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization.

9. International processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than the one in which you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. The latest version will always be posted on this page with a revised date.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy may be sent to support@imokfamily.com.

Important: IMOK is a family safety and notification service. It is not a medical device and does not replace emergency services or professional medical advice.
